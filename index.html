Rock climbing!


Although rock climbing can be visually cool, there are techniques and safety precautions to take before you climb!

Safety Checks:
  1. Double back your Harness Buckles
  2. Figure Eight Tie-In and Safety Knot
  3. Check your Belayer's Harness
  4. Check the Belay System
  5. Go Through the Starting Commands

Climbing Commands:
Position Command Purpose
Climber "On belay" Check the climber is on belay system
Belayer "Belay on" Climber is secure in the belay system and the belayer is ready to brake any falls
Climber "Climbing" Notifies the belayer you are no longer on the ground and your life is in their hands
Belayer "Climb on" The belayer acknowledges the start of the climb
